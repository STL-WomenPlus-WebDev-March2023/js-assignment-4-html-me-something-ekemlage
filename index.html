Teacher, Most Sacred Heart School, 2019-2023

Responsibilities:

Taught sixth grade math, seventh grade math (Pre-Algebra), and eighth grade math (Algebra 1) in addition to teaching preschool through eighth grade Spanish and an introductory programming course over Python, HTML, and Lego Mindstorm EV3 Robotics to middle school students.

Served as part of a committee to select a new mathematics curriculum for the school. Developed and sourced materials across grade levels for Spanish curriculum. Led weekly math advisory periods and weekly homeroom meetings with an emphasis on social and emotional growth.Differentiated instruction, teaching strategies, and learning activities across grade levels and subjects. Collaborated with co-teachers. Implemented and contributed to IEP’s. Co-sponsored school robotics club. Volunteered to work after school to tutor students.

Implemented school’s digital learning plan during suspension of in-person classes due to the Covid-19 pandemic. Taught in-person and online students. Tailored teaching strategies to support digital learners. Created digital content and videos to support distance learning.

Swim Instructor & Office Clerk, Foss Swim School, 2021

Responsibilities:

Explained products and services. Greeted clients. Answered phones and emails. Operated POS system. Taught students aged six months to adult swimming techniques and water safety skills to improve their swimming abilities and water confidence.

General Classroom Assistant, Wildwood Middle School, 2018-2019

Responsibilities:

Communicated closely with administrators and teachers. Worked with students, teachers, and administrators to provide classroom support. Developed rapport with students and coworkers. Assisted in a variety of subjects and grade levels. Served as a substitute teacher as needed. Aided in classroom management, instruction, and interventions. Worked individually and in small groups to provide additional or remedial instruction and assessment. Used Aleks program for math interventions to assess gaps in student knowledge, and address missing skills.

Artist, Hartmann's Art, 2015-2018

Responsibilities:

Worked as a henna/airbrush tattoo artist, face-painter, and cashier. Operated cash register. Interacted with the public. Explained and sold products. Developed painting and drawing skills. Opened and closed booth.

Summer Camp Planner & Counselor, St. Louis Science Center, 2016

Responsibilities:

Worked as part of a team to plan and lead ten different week long science themed summer day-camps for students aged four years to tenth grade. Interacted with parents, students, and staff and led groups of up to twenty-five students in teams of two.
